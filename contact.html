Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Maya AI and our services

What services does Maya AI offer?

Maya AI offers comprehensive AI solutions including AI audio generation with support for Indian languages, AI image creation, website development, mobile app development, and custom AI integration services for enterprises.

How long does it take to complete a project?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Simple AI integrations may take 2-4 weeks, while comprehensive website or mobile app development can take 8-16 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support and maintenance packages for all our solutions. This includes regular updates, bug fixes, performance optimization, and technical support to ensure your AI solutions continue to perform optimally.

Can Maya AI solutions be customized for specific industries?

Absolutely! Our AI solutions are highly customizable and can be tailored for various industries including healthcare, education, e-commerce, entertainment, finance, and more. We work closely with clients to understand their specific requirements.
